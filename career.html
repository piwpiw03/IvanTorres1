Career Highlights

  • 5× NBA champion (2000–2002, 2009, 2010)
  • 2× NBA Finals MVP (2009, 2010)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player (2008)
  • 18× NBA All-Star (1998, 2000–2016)
  • 4× NBA All-Star Game MVP (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)
  • 2× Olympic gold medalist (2008, 2012)
  • Scored 81 points in a single game (2006)
  • 4th all-time leading scorer in NBA history

Kobe's career was marked by his relentless work ethic, his clutch performances, and his impact both on and off the court. He was known for his "Mamba Mentality," a philosophy that encouraged perseverance and hard work.
